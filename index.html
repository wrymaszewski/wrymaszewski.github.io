Portfolio Apps

  • PlantPath DB

    Database interface for biological laboratory management. Users can register, log in, and add laboratory resources, manuals and expenses. Information is easily reclaimed using a friendly interface. This is a demo version, as the main release is in use by Laboratory of Plant Pathogenesis and contains sensitive information.

    Ruby on Rails, jQuery Datatables

  • Track Your Tops

    Ascent database for rock climbers. Users can create profiles and track both their indoor and outdoor ascents. The app is in development, but it already provides some simple statistics. Users can also join groups and socialize with others.

    Django, jQuery Datatables, GoogleMapsAPI, Highcharts

  • Veturilo Stats

    Statistics for the network of city bikes in Warsaw - Veturilo. The app scrapes Veturilo website in 10min intervals, stores the information about the availability of bicycles and performs data aggregation on a monthly basis. Machine learning module helping with future predictions is planned.

    Django, jQuery Datatables, GoogleMapsAPI, Pandas, Plotly, BeautifulSoup4

  • Shiny Apps

    A collection of small web apps written in R programming language and used for biological calculations.

    • ThermoFluor

      Calculation of protein melting temperatures (Tm) based on protein melting data from qPCR machines. Experimental designs and raw data are uses as input. The app annotates the samples, smoothes the melting curve, calculates Tm, and calculates means and SDs.

    • Peptide List Comparison

      Comparison of mass spectrometry lists of hits from MASCOT. The app take .xlsx files as input and produces both lists of intersections and Venn diagrams.

    • Competitive index

      Calculation and plotting of competitive index (CI), which is an analysis performed in plant pathogenesis research. The app takes either raw data or calculated CI in .xlsx format. It outputs calculated CI (if applicable), means and statistics, and adjustable boxplots and barplots.
